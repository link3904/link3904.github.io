WeMo Scooter

Project information

  • Category: Data Collection, Data Architecture, Business Analysis
  • Client: WeMo Scooter
  • Project date: September 2019 - January 2021
  • Project URL: www.wemoscooter.com

At WeMo, I assisted the company in building the data warehouse using Google Cloud's Big Query and Python ETL pipelines to push data from production to the warehouse. We utilized Apache Airflow as our task scheduler and manager for ETL pipelines, running on a Kubernetes Cluster in Google's Cloud Platform. On the top of the warehouse, we also built and maintained our own server for Metabase, which served as employees' access portal to data and business dashboards.

Besides from pulling production data, we also designed mechanisms of collecting data from in-app events using Firebase Analytics, as well as scraper bots that would collect data from the Central Weather Bureau for weather data and Google's Places API for POI data.

Lastly, I also participated in the Business Intelligence team that reported directly to the CEO for tackling critical business problems, such as increasing conversion rates through user acquisition funnels, optimizing vehicle downtime and vehicle distribution, and decreasing customer service tickets through research product feature recommendations.
